2 Pick a Portfolio Template That Converts

Once inside Wix dashboard, click "Templates" and filter by "Portfolio & CV". Avoid overly complex layouts. Look for these traits:

  • Large, high-quality image placeholders
  • Clear typography
  • Built-in contact form or social links

My top picks: "Creative Designer", "Photo Studio", or "Minimal CV". Click "Edit" — you're now in the live editor.

3 Customize in 5 Minutes (Images, Text, Colors)

Replace placeholder content with your own:

  • Hero section: Your name + tagline (e.g., "UI Designer & Illustrator")
  • Projects gallery: Drag your best 4–6 images or video links. No need for descriptions longer than one line.
  • About: Two sentences max — focus on the value you bring.
  • Contact: Add email and links to LinkedIn/Behance.

Wix's AI design assistant can suggest color palettes matching your brand. Use it — it's surprisingly good.

5 Optimize for Speed & SEO (1 Click)

Wix automatically generates a sitemap and basic SEO. Just go to "Settings → SEO" and edit your page title and meta description. Also, ensure images are compressed (Wix does this automatically). Then hit "Publish". That's it — your portfolio is live on the internet.
